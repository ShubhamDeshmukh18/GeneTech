Pioneers

GeneTech was developed by a team of bioinformaticians, clinicians, and software engineers with a shared goal to enhance the diagnosis of monogenic diabetes. Combining expertise in genetics and technology, they created a fast, secure platform that simplifies genetic data analysis while ensuring accuracy and data privacy.
